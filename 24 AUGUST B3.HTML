ALONE AGAIN

ONCE THERE WAS A BOY CALLED JOHNNY WHO HAD NO FRIENDS. ONE DAY THIS BOY CALLED ALLEN CAME AND STARTED TALKING TO JOHNNY. SOON JOHNNY AND ALLEN BECAME BEST FRIENDS. BUT JOHNNY NVER TOLD HIS PARENTS THAT HE HAS A FRIEND. POOR JOHNNY PARENTS WERE SAD BECAUSE THEY THOUGHT JOHNNY HAD NO FRIENDS. EVERYDAY AFTER SCHOOL ALLEN AND JOHNNY STARTED HANGING OUT. ONE DAY JOHNNY PARENTS SAID THAT THERE MOVING HOUSES. JOHNNY WAS SUPER SAD. THE NEXT DAY JOHNNY SAID BYE TO ALLEN MOVED HOUSES. JOHNNY AT HIS NEW HOME WAS VERY SAD SINCE HIS ONLY ALLEN WAS NOT THERE. WELL JOHNNY IS ALONE AGAIN.
