SLLEPING BOY

THERE ONCE WAS A BOY NAMED BARRY WHO SLEPT EVERY DAY. ONE DAY HIS PARENTS CAME AND WOKE HIM UP STRAIGHT AWAY. HE WOKE UP AND SAW HIS PARENTS WHO WERE VERY ANGRY AND GROUNDED HIM FOR 5 ENTIRE DAYS.AT SCHOOL HIS FRIENDS MISSED HIM A LOT SO HIS WROTE HIM A CARD AND GAVE TO HIS PARENTS. HIS PARENTS NEVER LIKED BARRY FRIENDS SO THEY NEVER GAVE THE CARD TO BARRY.WHEN BARRY RETURNED TO SCHOOL HIS FRIENDS ASKED HIM ABOUT THE CARD BUT BARRY WAS COMPLETELY CONFUSED.
